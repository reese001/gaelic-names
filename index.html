Gaelic Placenames in Nova Scotia, Canada

Many places in Nova Scotia have gaelic names besides english names. Located in communities such as Pictou, Antigonish, Guysborough, Inverness, Victoria, Richmond and the Cape Breton Regional Municipality, the following map marks these places.

Roll over the markers on the map to view the English and Gaelic names of locations throughout eastern Nova Scotia.
